🎬 After Effects Assembly Workflow

Step 1 — Create Composition: 1080 × 1920px, 30fps, total duration ~90–100 seconds

Step 2 — Import All 9 Images: Each image as a layer, duration as specified per scene above

Step 3 — Transition Between Scenes: Soft cross-dissolve (opacity fade) of 0.8 seconds between every scene

Step 4 — Add Text Per Scene: Use text specs above. All text goes in the TOP 55% of each frame (except Scene 9 which uses full frame)

Step 5 — Text Color Rule: ALWAYS use #1B3A5A (deep navy) or #2D1A0A (dark brown) for text. NEVER use cream, white, or light colors — they will be invisible on the cream background.

Step 6 — Font Install Required: Install "Great Vibes" and "Cormorant Garamond" fonts before opening AE. Download free from Google Fonts.

Step 7 — Audio: Add Indian classical / Shehnai BGM or soft folk music. Keep at 20% volume under full mix. Fade out last 3 seconds.
